Our Privacy Policy

We, the Arcadia High School App Development Team, operate this website, a news reader and digital toolkit for Arcadia High School students. By visiting this website, you are accepting and consenting to the practices described in this Policy. This Policy will govern our use of your information, regardless of what information we collect.

1. What information do we collect?

We only collect basic usage information in the form of logs from all visitors. When you visit this website, we log your IP address, the page you are visiting, and the time of your visit. We may also capture information about your computer system, such as your web browser and operating system types.

2. Why do we collect this information?

We collect this basic usage information in order to protect this website against malicious requests.

3. Changes

Our Privacy Policy may change pending the addition of new features. Should we ever choose to collect personal information, we will display the policy change prominently on this website.

This Privacy Policy was last updated on July 11th, 2021.

4. Definitions

IP address
A sequence of letters and numbers that can identify your computer network and its approximate geographic location.
Web browser
A computer application that reads and displays pages from the internet.
Operating system
A piece of software, such Windows or ChromeOS, that performs system tasks and provides basic services for your computer.
